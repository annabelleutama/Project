ABOUT ME

My name is Annabelle and I love all things beauty! I have loved beauty from a very young age and even remember watching makeup tutorials on YouTube ever since I turned 12. I spend most of my free time in Sephora online shopping and my friends know me as the "girl who has tried every product to ever be sold in Sephora". I decided to make this website to showcase a compiled list of products I have been trying and loving. I also will use this website to show my friends what products I use on the regular because they are always changing and I can just use this website as a compiled list of all my favorite products along with a detailed review. I am always getting makeup inspiration from Pinterest so I decided to include some of the beauty looks I would like to try in the near future, in the "Beauty Lookbook" page.
