Favorite Skincare and Hair Products

Cos Rx Advanced Snail 96 Mucin Power Essence

This product saved my skin barrier and is the perfect lightweight moisturizer for everyday use. I love how light it is and how it is unscented so it is perfect for anyone who has sensitive skin. I know it sounds quite offputting at first because they use snail mucin for this product, but don't knock it till you try it!

Cerave Foaming Facial Wash

I have tried so many different face washes for my oily skin and this is the only one that cleanses off the dirt and makeup accumulated throughout the day without drying my skin out. It is also sold in super large sizes which I love. It is also very affordable which is an added bonus. You just can't go wrong with this cleanser

Oribe Dry Texturizing Spray

This spray is so good at adding some volume to flat hair, I love how good it smells too! So many people have stopped to tell me how good I smell when it is just all because of this spray. It is quite expensive though, but I think it is very worth it because of how good it is.

Ouai Fine Hair Shampoo

I have very thick, heavy, straight and color treated hair so finding a good shampoo that will not ruin my color, and keep it volumized is very important to me, which is why I was so glad to find this product! It smells so good and I love how it is also sulfate-free making it color safe.

Kristin Ess Signature Conditioner

I love a good conditioner that keeps my hair smooth, tangle free, moisturized and smelling good. This one ticks all the boxes and also does not weigh down my already heavy hair! I love how this conditioner smells and the scent also lasts a really long time.
